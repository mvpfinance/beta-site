Services

MVP Finance e-Training

Student-athletes are granted 24/7 access to our online platform to learn and acquire financial knowledge about various finance-related topics. The program enforces learning material through a self-paced training component that uses whiteboard animation videos to invite interest and appeal to the aesthetic complex.

Smartphone Application

Our application is compatible with both Android and IOS devices. Features on the application include the option to create your own Financial Bucket List which allows each user to choose short-term and long-term financial goals that they want to work towards.

Interactive Workshop Construct

The face-to-face workshop process emphasizes a comprehensive financial framework into the athletic learning atmosphere. At MVP Finance, we firmly believe that all student-athletes benefit from using this program because they can ask relevant smart questions that we can provide answers to directly.

About

  • MVP Finance is a financial literacy program designed to help student-athletes take control of their financial future.

  • Using the Just-in-Time Teaching methodology (JiTT), the learning component of the program aims to effectively assess the needs of each individual student to ensure their success in their financial comprehension.

  • By categorizing each student by their class standing (i.e. freshman, sophomore, junior or senior status) the program utilizes relevant, age-appropriate material to foster each individual athlete’s understanding of finance.

  • The MVP Finance literacy program is unique in that it tailors the content of its workshops and classes to meet the specific needs of each student-athlete based on their level of class standing and achievement . Starting from basic financial concepts for long-term financial stability, this includes standard budgeting principles, the basics of how to build (and maintain) good credit, as well as helping to establish long term investment goals and strategies. In sum, MVP Finance combines a multisystemic approach to enable each student-athlete with the confidence and strategies necessary for long term monetary success in their future career goals.
